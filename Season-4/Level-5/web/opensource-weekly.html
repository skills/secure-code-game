The Conventional Commits specification reaches version 2.0, introducing first-class monorepo support with scoped changelogs and workspace-aware version bumping. Projects using Lerna, Nx, or Turborepo can now generate per-package release notes automatically.

Open Source Maintainer Burnout: New Study Reveals Key Factors

opensourcestudies.org · 6 min read

A comprehensive study of 2,500 open source maintainers reveals that unclear contribution guidelines and unmanaged expectations are the top contributors to burnout. The report recommends structured onboarding, contributor ladders, and sustainable funding models.

Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) Now Required for Federal Contracts

nist.gov · 3 min read

New federal regulations require all software vendors to provide SPDX or CycloneDX SBOMs for government contracts. Open source projects are encouraged to generate SBOMs as part of their release pipelines to ensure downstream compliance.
